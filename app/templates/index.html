12 Days of Parkmas – Summer Edition

Welcome to the Parkmas Scoring System! This event runs July 12–24 2026

Scoring is simple: activators earn points for each QSO made from a park, and hunters earn points for each QSO they log with an activator.

K0IRO 12 Days of Parkmas July Edition Contest Rules

Introduction: Activating in the parks is what keeps POTA alive — it gets radios into the outdoors, shows the hobby to the public, and builds skills. But none of it works without hunters at home; every QSO from the shack makes activations possible. This contest celebrates both sides: the activators braving the parks and the hunters who keep them connected.

Out in the Park

  • Base scoring: Each QSO = 2 points.
  • Day 1: 2 points per QSO.
  • Days 2–12: New park = 4 points per QSO; same park = 2 points per QSO. It’s a park a day — you can’t get all 12 parks on one day.
  • Modes: No band restriction (beyond normal license limits), but mode must be SSB or CW. FT, RTTY, and other data modes do not count.
  • QRP multiplier: 2× points for QRP activations (5 W or less) for the entire activation.
  • Operating location: Points don’t combine. Operate either from the QTH or from the park for a given activation. The spirit of this event is truly working from the park.
