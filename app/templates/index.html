12 Days of Parkmas – Summer Edition

Welcome to the Parkmas Scoring System! This event runs July 12–24 and tracks both activators in the parks and hunters operating from home. Scoring is simple: activators earn points for each QSO made from a park, and hunters earn points for each QSO they log with an activator. Full scoring details will be added here soon.

Use the navigation above to submit logs, view results, or manage your account. As logs come in, this page will display summaries, leaderboards, and event updates.
